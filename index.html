Welcome to the FarmLearn Study

Thank you for participating in this research study evaluating FarmLearn, a gamified mobile web application for agricultural education.

About This Study

This study investigates how gamification elements (levels, progress tracking, timed challenges, and immediate feedback) affect learning outcomes and user experience in agricultural education.

What You Will Do

  • Fill in a brief demographic questionnaire (2 min)
  • Use the FarmLearn application for approximately 10-15 minutes
  • Complete a post-use evaluation survey (5 min)
  • Answer a brief knowledge quiz (2 min)

Important Information

  • Total estimated time: 20-25 minutes
  • Your responses are anonymous and identified only by a participant code
  • There are no right or wrong answers in the survey sections
  • You may withdraw at any time without consequence
  • Data will be used solely for academic research purposes
